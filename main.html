A little about me.

I've made my home base at the western edge of the Sonoran Desert. My journey into tech started early as I earned an Electrical Engineering degree. As I started writing creative non-fiction, and sharing my voice online, I was fascinated with how easily I could get my own voice out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full-switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
