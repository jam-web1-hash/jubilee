I've made my home base the western end of the Sonoran Desert. My journey into tech started early. I got a degree in electrical engineering. As a hobby, I started sharing my writing online. I became fascinated with how easily I could get my voice out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my problem-solving and creative skills to write clean, elegant code.
